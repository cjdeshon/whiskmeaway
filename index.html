Our Story

WhiskMeAway was born from the love of home baking and the joy of sharing sweet treats with friends and family. Our founder grew up baking for firefighters across Greater Peoria, learning that the best recipes are made with a little sugar and a lot of heart. From those warm, flour-covered beginnings came a mission: bring that same feeling to every customer who opens a WhiskMeAway box.
